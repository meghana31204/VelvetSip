Brewing Guides

  • Espresso

    Start by heating your machine to brew espresso. Use finely ground coffee in the portafilter and pack it tightly. Brew for 25-30 seconds to get a strong shot topped with crema.

  • Americano

    Brew 1-2 shots of espresso, heat 120-180 ml of water, combine them in a cup, and adjust strength as desired to make an Americano.

  • Cappuccino

    A cappuccino is made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. Brew espresso, steam milk, pour over, and add foam on top for a rich, creamy coffee drink.

  • Latte

    A latte consists of espresso combined with steamed milk and topped with a light layer of milk foam. Brew 1-2 shots of espresso, steam about 240 ml of milk, and pour the milk over the espresso for a creamy

  • Mocha

    A mocha combines espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate syrup, topped with whipped cream. Brew espresso, mix with chocolate, pour steamed milk, and enjoy this sweet treat!

  • Macchiato

    A macchiato features espresso "stained" with a small amount of frothed milk. Brew a shot of espresso, add frothed milk

  • Flat White

    A flat white is made with espresso and steamed milk, featuring a velvety texture. Brew espresso, steam milk, and pour for a rich, creamy coffee experience.

  • Cortado

    This coffee drink combines equal parts espresso and steamed milk for a balanced flavor. Brew a shot of espresso, steam milk, and mix together for a rich taste.

  • Affogato

    A delightful dessert drink, affogato features a scoop of vanilla ice cream "drowned" in a shot of hot espresso. Pour espresso over the ice cream and enjoy!

  • Irish Coffee

    Irish coffee is made with brewed coffee, Irish whiskey, and sugar, topped with cream. Combine coffee and whiskey, add sugar, and float cream on top.
